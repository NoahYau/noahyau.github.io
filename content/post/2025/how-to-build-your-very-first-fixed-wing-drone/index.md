### What you will need:

- EPS/XPS/Depron foam (4mm)

- Aircraft plywood (2mm)

- Bamboo Skewers (3mm) \[The kind you can find from utensils\]

- Glue: UHU Por (Made for gluing polystyrene)

- Motor: XXD A2212 1400KV

- Propeller: 8060

- Servo: SG90 (9g) \*2

- ESC(Electronic Speed Controller): Anybrand 30A

- Receiver: any with ELRS protocol

- Transmitter (Remote Controller): e.g. Jumber T14 \[Running EdgeTX system\]

- Battery: LiPo 2200mAh 3s 11.1V

- Wheels: TBC

- Carbon Fiber Rods: 1mm

- Nylon Control Horn with Clevis
